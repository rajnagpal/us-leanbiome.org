Leanbiome Ingredients 

The ingredients of LeanBiome are meticulously chosen to offer a holistic approach to weight management and gut health. These components, backed by scientific research, come together to form a potent blend that not only aids in weight loss but also ensures a balanced gut microbiota. From probiotics to plant extracts, each ingredient plays a pivotal role in enhancing the body's natural ability to burn fat and maintain a healthy digestive system.

The ingredients used in the formation of Leanbiomeare mentioned below:

1

Greenselect Phytosome (green tea extract)

**Greenselect Phytosome** is a specialized green tea extract that offers the benefits of green tea in a more bioavailable form. It's known for its antioxidant properties and its ability to support weight management by enhancing metabolism and fat oxidation.
2

Lactobacillus Fermentum

**Lactobacillus Fermentum** is a type of probiotic bacteria that plays a crucial role in gut health. It's known to have beneficial effects on digestion and can also influence metabolism, potentially aiding in weight loss.
3

Lactobacillus gasseri

**Lactobacillus gasseri** is another probiotic strain that's been linked to various health benefits, especially concerning the gut. It's been associated with weight loss, improved digestion, and a reduction in belly fat.
4

L. Rhamnosus

**L. Rhamnosus** is a beneficial bacteria naturally found in the gut. Clinical studies have shown its potential in aiding weight loss, especially when combined with a balanced diet and regular exercise.
5

Inulin

**Inulin** is a type of prebiotic fiber that promotes the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. It aids in digestion, helps in nutrient absorption, and can have a positive impact on bowel regularity.
6

Chromium

**Chromium** is a trace mineral that plays a vital role in macronutrient metabolism. It helps in the regulation of blood sugar levels and can support weight management by influencing carbohydrate, fat, and protein metabolism.
7

Glucomannan

**Glucomannan** is a dietary fiber derived from the root of the konjac plant. It's known for its ability to promote feelings of fullness, thereby reducing calorie intake and supporting weight loss.
8

Lactobacillus strains

**Lactobacillus strains** are a group of beneficial bacteria that play a pivotal role in maintaining gut health. They help in digestion, nutrient absorption, and can also influence mood and overall well-being.
9

Probiotic bacteria

**Probiotic bacteria** are live microorganisms that offer numerous health benefits when consumed in adequate amounts. They help in maintaining a balanced gut microbiota, which is essential for digestion, immunity, and overall health.
10

Prebiotic fibres

**Prebiotic fibres** are non-digestible food ingredients that promote the growth of beneficial gut bacteria. They play a crucial role in maintaining gut health, supporting digestion, and providing a source of energy for the beneficial bacteria in the gut.
